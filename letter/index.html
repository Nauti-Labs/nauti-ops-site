FAQ

Where does the data come from?
AIS vessel positions from aisstream.io, tides and currents from NOAA CO-OPS, and USCG notice data. Pro adds Nauti-Labs curation and desk-friendly formatting. Custom plans can include additional licensed or client-specific inputs.
Who is this for?
Vessel agents, ship brokers, terminal ops, P&I clubs, commodity traders with Gulf exposure, and anyone who needs Houston Ship Channel situational awareness without building it themselves.

Do you cover Galveston / Texas City / Freeport?

No. Nauti-Labs is independent. Free and Pro use public and operator-curated sources. Any premium pilot or terminal data would be handled as a separately scoped custom engagement.
